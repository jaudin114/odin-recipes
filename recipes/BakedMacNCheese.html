Baked Mac n Cheese

Description

A fun and simple recipe for a very tasty dish!

Ingredients

Macaroni
  • Unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 pound elbow macaroni
Cheese Sauce
  • 1/2 stick of unsalted butter
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups grated sharp cheddar

Steps

  1. Macaroni:Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Macaroni:Bring 4 quarts of water to a boil in a saucepan. Add the salt and macaroni.Cook the macaroni for 12 minutes until tender. Drain water.
  3. Cheese Sauce:Melt the butter in a 1 quart saucepan.Using a wire whisk, stir in the flour and salt, stirring and cooking over medium heat until the roux bubbles and the flour is a pale brown, about 3 minutes.
  4. Cheese Sauce:Carefully whisk in 1 cup of milk and the whisk in the remaining 1 cup milk. Continue to stir until sauce thickens
  5. Cheese Sauce: Add the cheese and stir until it melts. Add the drained macaroni to the cheese sauce and mix
  6. Bake until dish bubbles around the edges.(about 15 minutes)
