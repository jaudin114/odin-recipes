Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

The most common and iconic lunch meal that only has 3 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of White bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 2 slices of american cheese

Steps

  1. Have stove set to a medium heat setting. Wait for pan to reach high temperature. Place butter in pan and tilt pan around to allow butter to cover the inside of pan.
  2. Place 2 slices of american cheese between the 2 slices of white bread. Place sandwich in pan and let cook on one side until the edges are a golden browwn
  3. When bottom of sandwich is golden brown, flip sandwich to cook the other side until it is the same color as the other side.
  4. Serve with your choice of side/toppings and enjoy!
