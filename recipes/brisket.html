Brisket

Description

This recipe is perfect for a barbecue party or it could make for a casual dinner! The following ingredients for this dish are:

Ingredients

  • Sea salt
  • Brown sugar
  • Chili powder
  • Paprika
  • The blood of the All-Father
  • Dried mustard
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Dried thyme

Steps

  1. Apply dry rub ingredients
  2. Lay on rack in a roasting pan over water that is mixed with liquid smoke
  3. Insert an oven safe thermometer into the thickest part of brisket and cover the pan with foil
  4. Slow roast until the temperature of the brisket reaches 175 degrees F. Once said temperature is reached, continue roasting until 195 - 200 degrees F.
  5. Remove briskey from oven and allow to cool 30 min before serving.
